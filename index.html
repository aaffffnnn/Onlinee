How it works

Three steps, from first call to running system.

01 / Scope

We map what's actually needed

A working session to identify which systems, roles, and processes are worth taking off your plate first — no bundled packages you don't need.

02 / Deploy

We build and staff it

Infrastructure gets built, people get hired and trained on your product specifically, and operating processes get documented.

We operate it, you review it

ONLINEE runs day-to-day execution. You get regular reporting and stay in control of direction, without being in the weeds.
